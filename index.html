About ME

Almost everyone in this world is associated with design to some extent. Color choices for our clothes, rooms, phone cases along with the preferences of different shapes are all part of the creative design process. Hi, I am Kishor Karki. I am from Kathmandu, Nepal. I am a designer and a front end web developer. I love what I do and I adore the creative process. It is addictive and can give never ending possibilities.

I have completed my Bachelors in Computer Science and Information Information Technology (BSc. CSIT) from Amrit Science Campus. I worked as an Indesign Artist from April 2019 to August 2022. Mostly, I like to spend time with my personal projects. Apart from my professional expertise, I like to play guitar, listen music and watch movies in my spare time. I enjoy going out to get fresh air to boost my creative mind.

I am actively invoved in numerous sites like FreeCodeCamp, Front End Mentor, CSS Battles, Codepen and much more for enhancing my CSS and Javascript skills . These sites, in my opinion are very useful for testing and improving your skills.
